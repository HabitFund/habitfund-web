Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

HabitFund ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

No Personal Data Collection

HabitFund is designed to be used without any user registration or account creation. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information that can identify you, such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.

Usage Statistics (Analytics)

To provide a better user experience and improve app functionality, we use Google Analytics for Firebase. This service collects anonymous, aggregated data, including:

  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics (feature engagement, session duration)
  • General geographic location (city/country level, not precise GPS)

This data is entirely anonymous and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Data Storage and Syncing

All habit-related data and progress you enter are stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, your data is securely synchronized across your devices via your own Apple iCloud account. We (the developers) do not have access to, nor do we store your personal habit data on our own servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app utilizes the following third-party service provider for analytics:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase: Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
